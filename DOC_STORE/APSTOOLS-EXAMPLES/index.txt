Examples
********

Examples show the steps that demonstrate how something was done, often
with minimal explanation.

In addition to the examples presented below, there are many Jupyter
notebooks online to provide additional Bluesky training for the APS.
Further, NSLS-II provides online notebooks which can be executed
online (in a web browser). See the Bluesky home page for more details.
